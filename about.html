What this site is

This is my public workshop. After two decades of building and running IT for holding companies, telecoms, banks-owned investment firms and entertainment groups across Kuwait, Malaysia and India, I've learned that the useful knowledge rarely lives in the vendor documentation — it lives in what actually happened on the project. So I write it down: honest, first-person guides on infrastructure, project delivery, cyber security and the certifications that map them.

It's written for working IT professionals and technology leaders, and doubles as study-companion material for people chasing the same certifications I hold. If you read something here and it saves you a week, the site has done its job.

What I actually do

  • IT infrastructure & operations — designing, building and running environments end to end: Active Directory, Exchange, virtualisation, backup (Veeam), endpoint and email security (Bitdefender, Proofpoint), Cisco networking, monitoring and hardening.
  • Project & programme management — leading IT projects and portfolios to scope, schedule and budget across multiple countries; PMP, PRINCE2 and agile in practice, not just on paper.
  • Cyber security, audit & risk — the ISACA disciplines (CISA, CISM, CRISC) plus the attacker's and investigator's mindset from CEH and CHFI, applied to real estates and audits.
  • Cloud & the Microsoft stack — Azure infrastructure, Microsoft 365 administration and cybersecurity architecture; migrations, cost optimisation and security baselines.
  • Enterprise architecture — TOGAF-grounded thinking that keeps technology tied to what the business is actually trying to do.
